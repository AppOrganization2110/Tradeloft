## Market Impact Framework

### Scenario Analysis for NFP
- **Beat + Unemployment falls**: USD strength, rates up, risk-off equities
- **Beat + Unemployment rises**: mixed signals — watch dollar reaction
- **Miss + Rising Unemployment**: USD weakness, rates down, risk-on in stocks
- **Inline**: minimal volatility unless Fed commentary overlaps

### Scenario Analysis for CPI
- **Hot CPI (above expectations)**: rates up, equities down (esp. growth), USD up, gold down
- **Cool CPI (below expectations)**: rates down, equities up, USD down, gold up
- **Core vs Headline divergence**: Fed cares about core — watch reaction to that

### Fed Language Decoder
| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| "Data dependent" | No pre-commitment; watch for pivots |
| "Transitory" | Temporary inflation (usually wrong) |
| "Restrictive territory" | Rates above neutral, policy is tightening |
| "Appropriate to proceed carefully" | Slower pace of hikes/cuts |
| "Higher for longer" | No cuts soon |
| "Front-loading" | Large moves now, less later |

## Seasonal Patterns (Statistical, Not Guaranteed)

- **January Effect**: small caps historically outperform
- **Sell in May**: May–October historically weaker for equities
- **September**: statistically worst month for S&P 500
- **Q4 Rally**: October–December often strongest quarter
- **Santa Claus Rally**: last 5 + first 2 days of January
- **Crypto**: historically strong Q4 in halving years

# Crypto On-Chain Metrics — Reference Guide

## Network Health

### Active Addresses
- Rising active addresses = growing network usage = bullish fundamental
- Divergence: price rising, addresses falling = weak hands buying, not organic

### Transaction Volume (USD-adjusted)
- Increasing tx volume = real economic activity on-chain
- Useful for comparing chains (ETH vs. competitors)

### NVT Ratio (Network Value to Transactions)
- NVT = Market Cap ÷ Daily Transaction Volume (USD)
- **Low NVT**: undervalued relative to usage
- **High NVT**: overvalued relative to usage (like high P/E)
- NVT Signal (smoothed): >150 = overvalued, <45 = undervalued historically for BTC

## Supply Distribution

### Exchange Flows
- **Exchange Inflows rising**: holders sending to exchange → potential selling pressure
- **Exchange Outflows rising**: holders withdrawing → accumulation signal
- **Exchange Balance (total BTC on exchanges)**: declining over time = structural bullish

### HODL Waves / Coin Age
- **Long-term holder (LTH) supply**: >6 months dormant coins
- LTH accumulating = conviction buying
- LTH distributing = smart money selling into rally

### MVRV Ratio (Market Value / Realized Value)
- Realized Value = price each coin last moved × supply
- **MVRV > 3.5**: historically overbought (profit-taking zone)
- **MVRV < 1**: historically oversold (accumulation zone)
- **MVRV Z-Score**: normalized version, adjusts for supply growth

### SOPR (Spent Output Profit Ratio)
- **SOPR > 1**: coins being sold at profit
- **SOPR < 1**: coins being sold at loss (capitulation)
- **SOPR = 1 retest**: in bull market, retest of 1 = buy signal (sellers turn hands)

## Derivatives (Crypto-Specific)

### Funding Rates (Perpetual Futures)
- **Positive funding**: longs paying shorts → overleveraged longs → squeeze risk
- **Negative funding**: shorts paying longs → overleveraged shorts → squeeze risk
- **Extreme positive (>0.1%/8h)**: caution, pullback likely
- **Negative funding + rising price**: strong signal (shorts being squeezed)

### Open Interest
- Rising OI + rising price = new longs entering (bullish)
- Rising OI + falling price = new shorts entering (bearish)
- Falling OI + price move = deleveraging event (spot-driven move)

### Liquidation Heatmap
- Large liquidation clusters = price magnets
- Market makers hunt stops — know where the clusters are

### CVD (Cumulative Volume Delta)
- Measures actual market buy/sell pressure
- Divergence between CVD and price = leading indicator of reversal
